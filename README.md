# Retail_Business

Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis
Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to evaluate revenue performance, profit contribution, and margin trends across categories, regions, and time periods.
The objective was to identify key profit drivers, understand seasonal performance patterns, and evaluate category-level margin efficiency.

Dataset


Key Analyses Performed
1. Revenue & Profit Contribution

Calculated revenue contribution by month and category
Calculated profit contribution by month and category
Compared revenue share vs profit share to assess margin efficiency

2. Profit Margin Trend

Analyzed monthly gross profit margin trends
Identified periods of improved profitability

3. Category-Level Performance

Evaluated high-revenue vs high-margin categories
Identified categories driving overall profitability

Key Insights

Revenue and profit both increased in the later months, indicating seasonal demand impact.
Profit margins improved alongside revenue growth, suggesting profitable expansion rather than discount-driven sales.
Certain categories contributed disproportionately to total profit due to stronger margin efficiency.
